Magnet Copier

Copy magnet:// links on iOS and visionOS

Have you ever encountered a magnet:// link in your iOS or visionOS browser, and wish you had an easy way to copy them to your clipboard?

Magnet Copier is the app you've been looking for! Tap the magnet link, open it in this app, and then press the "Copy to Clipboard" button. It's that easy!

Once the link is in your clipboard, what you do with it is up to you! Paste it wherever you want!
